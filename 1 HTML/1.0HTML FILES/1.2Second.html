The abhishek reciepe

ingrediant

  • 1 liter milk
  • 150gram rice
  • 20gram dry fruits
  • 2gram kesar

Instruction to make kheer

  1. Take a bowl and put it into the gas
  2. Put the milk into the bowl
  3. Boil it in low flame
  4. When the milk start boiling put rice into it
  5. After some time put all the dry fruits into the milk
  6. check if the rice is fully cook the put the kesar and close the gas
